About Us

Here at The Flying Row, we are a collective of barbers and hair stylists, whom focus on haircuts that match each individual. We pride ourselves for giving excellent service, as well as a tailored experience for each of our clients.

Also, we strongly believe in listening to client's needs and explaining steps in a relaxing manner.

Please check us out and we hope to meet you soon! Thank you!
